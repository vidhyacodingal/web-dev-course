About Queens court school

Queens Court School is a private basic school located in Adjei-Kojo, a suburb of Tema in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. Founded in 2004 by Mrs. Ivy Ahiabor, the school aims to provide high-quality education in a nurturing environment. It follows a both GES and British-based curriculum from Kindergarten to Grade 9 and is accredited by the Ghana Education Service


The school focuses on holistic education, emphasizing not only academic subjects but also character development, creative arts, and sports. It offers various extracurricular activities like the Queens Court Choir, Drama Club, and Sports Club. The school prides itself on creating a nurturing environment where children can be themselves and reach their full potential. The dedicated teachers provide individualized attention and foster a warm and supportive learning atmosphere.


Queens Court School is highly regarded in Ghana and consistently ranks among the top private schools in the country. It has a strong reputation for academic excellence and is recognized for its commitment to character development and social responsibility
